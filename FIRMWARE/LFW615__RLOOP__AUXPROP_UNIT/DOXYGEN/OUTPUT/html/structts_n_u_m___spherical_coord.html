Detailed Description

Spherical coordinates (r, theta, phi) as often used in mathematics: radial distance r, azimuthal angle theta, and polar angle phi.

The meanings of theta and phi have been swapped compared to the physics convention. https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spherical_coordinate_system
